CGEM Telescope

System collecting pointing data for the telescope with an STM32 H723ZG microcontroller.

Mechanical setup to validate a 20-bit absolute Hohner Encoder.

Zhen Lab (computational neuroscience research)

CaTracker software uses an LSTM neural network to track neurons in calcium-imaged videos. I developed the GUI (and drew the pink cats!).

A poster of my research project quantifying the stress-induced neuron structural changes in early-development. I won 'best research' awards at Molecular Genetics/Biochem/Data Science Institute research showcases!

Buger-Cooking Robot

Meet Bernadette, our burger-making robot! Features encoded arms with deconstructed vacuum cleaners, numerous sensors, and three microcontrollers communicating via Bluetooth and UART.

Self-Driving Robot

Simple line-following using foundational computer vision concepts.

Intermediate line-following with ROS in a Gazebo environment.
